NETWORK DIAGNOSTICS

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 25, 2026 · Effective: April 25, 2026
Summary: Specter is a local network diagnostic tool. It scans your own WiFi network. All scan data stays on your device. We do not collect, store or sell your network data or personal information.

1. WHO WE ARE

Specter Network Diagnostics is developed and published by Obsidian Studio Designs, based in South Africa.

Contact: obsidianstudiodesigns@gmail.com

2. WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information collected locally on your device (never sent to us):

  • IP addresses of devices on your local network
  • MAC addresses of devices on your local network
  • Device hostnames and vendor names
  • Open network ports on scanned devices
  • Network signal strength readings
  • Scan history you choose to save

Information collected for subscription management:

  • A randomly generated device identifier (used to manage your subscription tier)
  • Your subscription status (Free, Home, or Pro)
  • App version number
  • Date of first app install (for trial period tracking)

Information we never collect:

  • Passwords or credentials of any network devices
  • Contents of network traffic
  • Personal files or documents
  • Location data
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Camera or microphone data

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

The device identifier and subscription status are used solely to:

  • Determine which features you have access to (Free, Home, or Pro tier)
  • Manage your 14-day free trial period
  • Process and verify your subscription payments via Google Play

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

All network scan data is stored locally on your device only. You control this data and can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Subscription management data is stored securely on our server hosted on Railway.app with industry-standard encryption.

5. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties, except:

  • Google Play Billing — processes your subscription payments. Google's privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • Railway.app — hosts our licence server. Only your device ID and subscription status are stored here.

6. PERMISSIONS USED

  • INTERNET — required to perform network scans and contact our licence server
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — required to detect your current WiFi network
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required to detect network connectivity
  • CHANGE_WIFI_MULTICAST_STATE — required for device discovery on local network

We do not request permissions for contacts, camera, microphone, location, or storage beyond what is listed above.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Specter is a professional network diagnostic tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your device ID and subscription data by emailing us
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play
  • Delete all local scan data by clearing the app's storage

9. RETENTION

Subscription data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon cancellation, data is retained for 30 days then permanently deleted. Local scan data on your device is never sent to us and is deleted when you clear app data or uninstall.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use of Specter after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
