COMMON QUESTIONS
Is this a monthly subscription payment or subscription?
Specter uses simple monthly subscriptions. You're billed automatically each month via PayFast. Cancel anytime — no contracts, no penalties. When you cancel, your access continues until the end of the billing period.
How do I receive my APK after payment?
After payment confirmation we will send your licensed APK directly to your email within 24 hours.
Can I upgrade from Home to Pro later?
Yes. Contact us and we will charge you the difference — R400 — to upgrade from Home to Pro at any time.
Does Specter work without internet?
Yes. Specter runs fully offline on your local network. No internet connection required to scan.
What Android version is required?
Specter requires Android 8.0 or higher. No root required. Works on any Android phone or tablet.
